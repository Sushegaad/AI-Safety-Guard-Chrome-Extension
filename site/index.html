Features

Calm protection that stays out of the way.

Local scanning

Analyzed on your device, never stored or uploaded.

Pre-send warning

Lists exactly what was found — masked values only.

Live risk badge

A small badge near the input updates as you type.

One-click redaction

Swaps secrets for labels like [EMAIL] and [API_KEY].

Attachment scanning

Reads PDF & Word text locally, comments and metadata too.

Three sensitivity modes

Basic, Balanced and Strict, switchable any time.

Shield Mode

Optional private composer: sites can't read your draft as you type — text stays yours until you approve it.

Per-site control

Turn watching on or off per site; add custom domains.

Accessible

Keyboard operable, screen-reader ready. WCAG 2.1 AA & Section 508.

Least privilege

Runs only on supported AI sites; requests only what it uses.
